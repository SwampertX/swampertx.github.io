Linux Wired Earphones White Noise Solution

Mon, Jan 1, 0001

There was white noise.

This is a short write-up on how I solve the issue in the title.

How I solved it

I went to alsamixer and choose the correct sound card (HDA Intel PCH).

Then press right until you see the Auto-Mute option. Enable it.

That solved the issue for me.

Some descriptions for the original problem.

There was white noise whenever I plugged in my earphones since a few weeks ago.

There was no such white noise in my bluetooth earbuds and from speaker, so it is unlikely a kernel/sound card issue.

I read online that the sound might go away if the laptop is not charging. Indeed, sometimes the idle white noise go away when unplugged, but appears again when any media is played.

Updating BIOS didn't work. Changing from PulseAudio to PipeWire also did not work.

Finally, this solution worked for me. No perceived problem yet. Playbacks sound clear now.
